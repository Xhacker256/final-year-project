About Us

We Are Sannyu Tyre Centre — your trusted destination for high-quality tyres and rims.

With years of experience in the automotive industry, we specialize in providing premium wheels and tyres for all types of vehicles — from everyday commuters to high-performance machines. Whether you're after reliability, style, or performance, we’ve got the perfect fit for your ride.

At the heart of our business is a passion for quality and customer satisfaction. We work with top local and international brands to ensure every product we offer meets the highest standards for safety, durability, and design.

What sets us apart?

  • Expert Guidance – Our knowledgeable team is always ready to help you choose the right tyres and rims based on your vehicle, driving habits, and budget.
  • Competitive Pricing – We offer some of the best prices in the market without compromising on quality.
  • Fast & Reliable Service – From selection to fitting, we make the process smooth, professional, and efficient.
  • After-Sales Support – We stand by our products and are here to support you even after the sale.


Whether you're upgrading your look, replacing worn-out tyres, or seeking better performance, we’re here to make it easy and affordable.

Come visit us in-store or browse our collection online — and drive away with confidence.
